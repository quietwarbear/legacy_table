# Legacy Table — AI Video Script Prompt

*Use this prompt with an AI video generation tool (Synthesia, HeyGen, Runway, Pika, etc.) to create a promotional/walkthrough video for Legacy Table.*

---

## PROMPT FOR AI VIDEO MAKER

**Video Title:** Legacy Table — Preserve Your Family's Recipes Forever

**Duration:** 60-90 seconds

**Tone:** Warm, heartfelt, modern. Think Sunday dinner energy meets clean tech design. Speak to someone who loves their family and wants to keep traditions alive. Not corporate — personal.

**Visual Style:** Warm color palette (ambers, creams, rich browns). Show a mix of cozy kitchen scenes, family gatherings around food, hands holding recipe cards, and clean UI screenshots of the app on phone screens. Transitions should feel smooth and cinematic, not flashy.

**Target Audience:** Adults 25-55 who cook family recipes and want to preserve them for the next generation. Parents, grandchildren, anyone who treasures food traditions.

**Music:** Gentle acoustic guitar or piano. Warm and inviting, not sad. Builds slightly during the feature showcase, then settles back down for the close. Think Sunday morning, not Monday meeting.

### PRODUCTION NOTES FOR AI VIDEO TOOLS

**If using Synthesia or HeyGen (avatar-based):**
- Use a warm, friendly narrator avatar. Ideally someone who looks like they enjoy cooking — approachable, not corporate.
- Background should be a clean kitchen or warm living space, not an office.
- Have the avatar gesture naturally toward screen recordings when showing app features.

**If using Runway, Pika, or Sora (generative video):**
- Generate the kitchen/family scenes as cinematic B-roll.
- For app UI shots, use actual screenshots from the app overlaid on phone mockups.
- Use smooth Ken Burns-style movements on still images.
- Keep generated people diverse and multi-generational — this is a family app.

**If combining tools:**
- Generate B-roll scenes with Runway/Pika.
- Record actual app UI as screen captures on a phone or emulator.
- Composite together in CapCut, DaVinci Resolve, or similar.
- Add voiceover separately using ElevenLabs or similar for the warmest, most natural sound.

**Screen recordings needed:**
1. Home screen with recipe grid
2. A single recipe detail page (showing story, photos, ingredients)
3. The share modal — cycling through themes, switching to Story format
4. The Family page — Quick Start card and the tap-to-seed flow
5. The pricing page showing both tiers
6. The credit badge in the navigation bar

**Recommended voiceover style:**
- Male or female, mid-30s to 50s
- Warm baritone or warm alto
- Conversational, not announcer-style
- Slight smile in the voice — like they're telling you about something they genuinely love
- Pace: unhurried but not slow. Let moments breathe.
