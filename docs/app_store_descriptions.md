### Description

Your family's recipes are more than instructions — they're memories, traditions, and love passed down through generations. Legacy Table is the only app built to preserve the full story behind every dish.

**Save recipes the way your family tells them.** Add the ingredients, the steps, the photos — and the story. Who made it first? What holiday does it belong to? What does it mean to your family? Legacy Table keeps all of it together, beautifully.

**Share with your whole family.** Create a family group and invite everyone. Grandma's handwritten recipe card, Dad's famous jerk chicken, Auntie's sweet potato pie — they all live in one place, accessible to every family member.

**Beautiful share cards for social media.** Choose from 5 styled themes — Heritage, Midnight, Garden, Spice, and Ivory — and export in Square (1:1) or Instagram Story (9:16) format. Recipe photos become stunning backgrounds automatically. Share your family's food culture with the world.

**AI-powered features with monthly credits.** Every account gets free AI credits each month. Use them to scan handwritten recipe cards, convert voice recordings to recipes, and more features coming soon. Paid plans unlock more credits for families who cook often.

**Quick Start in seconds.** New to Legacy Table? Tap one button to create your family and get sample recipes instantly. See exactly how the app works, then replace them with your own.

**Two plans for every family:**

Heritage Keeper ($9.99/mo or $99.99/yr) — 15 AI credits/month, unlimited recipe storage, family sharing for up to 10 members, photo uploads, recipe export, and categories.

Legacy Collection ($19.99/mo or $199.99/yr) — 50 AI credits/month, everything in Heritage Keeper plus unlimited family members, advanced organization, priority support, early access to new features, and custom cookbook themes.

Free accounts can store recipes, join a family, and get 3 AI credits per month to try premium features.

**Your recipes. Your family. Your legacy.**
